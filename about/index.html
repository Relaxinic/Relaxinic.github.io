About Rinic

Currently

Rinic is an undergraduate student in Computer Science at Zhejiang University (Class of 2028). His work and interests focus on the intersection of artificial intelligence and music, particularly in areas such as music information retrieval, generative music systems, and human–AI interaction in creative practices.

About this site

This website serves as a personal research and writing space. Here he documents technical experiments, research notes, and small projects related to AI‑driven music technologies. The site also collects occasional writings on music, culture, and listening.

Research Interests

  • Music Information Retrieval (MIR)
  • Human-AI Music Interaction (HCI)
  • Multimodal Music Understanding
