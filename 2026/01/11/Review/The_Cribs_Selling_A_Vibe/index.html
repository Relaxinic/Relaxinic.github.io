Selling A Vibe The Cribs

rating: ★★★

mark:

Dark Luck Never The Same Self-Respect

The production of the vocals deliberately retains a gritty rock texture with a sense of distance, yet the melodic lines are crafted quite smoothly, with plenty of clever details. The guitar tones are bright and crisp, allowing the entire work to strike a delicate balance between roughness and sweetness. Overall, it is a well-executed “sophisticated little sweetener.”

人声的制作刻意保留了一种带距离感的硬地摇滚质地，但旋律线条却写得相当流畅，细节里的巧思也不少；吉他的音色明亮而干脆，让整张作品在粗粝与甜度之间取得了一种微妙的平衡。整体来说，是一张完成度不错的“高级小甜水”。
