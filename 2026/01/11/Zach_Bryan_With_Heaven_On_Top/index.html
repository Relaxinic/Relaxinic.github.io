With Heaven On Top Zach Bryan

rating: ★★★

mark:

Drowning South and Pine Slicked Back

To some extent, this advantage is also highlighted by comparison with peers: whether in terms of lyrics, melodies, or instrumentation, his works possess a fresher and more sincere quality compared to many typical American male country singers. It is evident that his roots in music do not entirely belong to traditional country but carry a stronger folk flavor, somewhat reminiscent of artists like Bon Iver. However, no matter how refreshing the writing style, packing 25 songs into one album still feels somewhat excessive. Of course, considering his inclination toward commercial-oriented creation, such a scale of tracks is likely driven by market considerations.

某种程度上也算是靠同行衬托出来的优势：无论是歌词、旋律还是配器，相比许多典型的美国乡村男歌手，他的作品里多了一种更清爽、也更真诚的气质。能明显听出他的根源音乐并不完全属于传统的 country，而是带着更浓一点的 folk 气息，多少会让人联想到 Bon Iver 一类的路径。不过再怎么清爽的写作风格，一张专辑一下塞进 25 首歌还是显得有些过量。当然，考虑到他本身也偏向商业向创作，这样的曲目规模大概也是出于市场层面的考量。
