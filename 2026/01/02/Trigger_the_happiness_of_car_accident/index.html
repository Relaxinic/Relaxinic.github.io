车祸的幸福 – Trigger

rating: ★★★★

mark:
幸福的车祸 | 地球失去意识 | 美瞳 | 日夜呢喃的惯犯 | 狙击 | 永久地走掉 | 烧起来、舞蹈、浇灭 | 悲伤盒子

comment:
The guitar arrangement is so beautiful it almost brings tears, and the melodies seem more pleasing to the ear compared to nono’s debut album. However, does it feel a bit weak in the latter half? If “Burn, Dance, Extinguish” were placed earlier, I think I would have fallen in love with it. // Second edit: Now it doesn’t feel weak anymore, with many new highlights added. Isn’t this the best album since the start of the year? It’s concise yet powerful. The vocal arrangement leans toward instrumentalization, which isn’t entirely new but is praiseworthy. Interestingly, the electric guitar and nono’s bright vocal line never seem to clash; it’s always clear who takes the low or high frequencies and who supports or leads (which also seems to prove the instrumentalization of the vocal arrangement).

吉他编排好听得想哭，旋律好像也比nono首专顺耳了一些，但是听到后半段有点乏力了？如果把《烧起来、舞蹈、浇灭》放更前面我想我会喜欢上。//二编：现在听已经不会乏力了，新增了很多mark。这难道不是开年以来最好的专辑吗？足够精简也足够有力。人声编排偏于器乐化，不算新但是很值得夸的方向。有意思的是电吉他和nono的亮声线似乎从没有打架过，每个时候谁低频谁高频、谁辅助谁主导分得很清楚（好像也是人声编排器乐化的证明）。
