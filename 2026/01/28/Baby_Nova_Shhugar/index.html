Shhugar Baby Nova

rating: ★★☆

mark:

Broke Bitch Boogie Virgin Heartbreak Wonder Bread Winner

Post-Chappell Roan-style pop artists can be summarized as follows: they employ a creative approach that blends elements such as pop dance music and country, attempting to carve out a seemingly “new” expressive path through highly empathetic narratives, yet ultimately falling back into the traditional pop logic of relying on melody to succeed. She currently aligns more closely with a diluted version of this paradigm—her style and packaging are already in place, but there is a noticeable deficiency in the core melody writing (especially in constructing hooks and memorable points), resulting in a listening experience that remains at the atmospheric level and lacks specific, memorable passages to revisit. After listening to the entire album, emotions are stirred, but the melodies themselves are difficult to recall. However, considering this is her debut album and the creative framework is not yet fully solidified, this state of “expression first, melody lagging” still has room for adjustment, and her future development is worth continued observation.
