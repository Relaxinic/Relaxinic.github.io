XO, My Cyberlove CHUU

rating: ★☆

mark:

XO, My Cyberlove Cocktail Dress

Perhaps I shouldn’t have placed some of my expectations for LOONA on her. If paired with visuals, I think she could be a sweet female idol with a top-tier audiovisual experience, but when it comes to music alone, the arrangement still feels timid and ultimately tends to be conservative. She doesn’t explore synthesizers to the extent LOONA does, and her experimentation with genres is superficial and only scratches the surface. The lyrics are still quite dull, though I never had high hopes for them in the first place.

也许我就不应该将本月少女的一部分期望寄托在她身上。如果配合视觉我想是一个视听体验上乘的甜系女爱豆，但是只论音乐的话，编曲依旧畏手畏脚最终显得趋于保守，玩合成器也玩不到本月少女的程度，玩曲风也浅尝辄止、浮于表面。歌词依旧很无聊，不过我也没在这上面抱过太大希望。
