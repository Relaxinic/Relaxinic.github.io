Secret Love - Dry Cleaning

rating: ★★★

mark:

Hit My Head All Day Secret Love (Concealed in a Drawing of a Boy) Let Me Grow and You'll See the Fruit Evil Evil Idiot The Cute Things

The gloomy and somber post-punk atmosphere is built upon a rather meticulous production, but the predominantly spoken-word vocals come across as somewhat bland. The most captivating moments of the entire album almost entirely stem from the dense textures laid out by the guitar and bass: sweeping strums and low-frequency lines form the true emotional backbone. Overall, it is not a bad work, but it still falls a bit short of the expected “wallpaper-style” album that one could listen to on repeat.

阴郁而暗沉的后朋克气质建立在相当细致的制作之上，但以念白为主的 vocal 多少显得有些寡淡。整张专辑最动听的时刻，几乎都来自吉他与贝斯铺陈出的厚重纹理：大面积的扫弦与低频线条构成了真正的情绪支撑。整体来说，它并不是一张糟糕的作品，但距离预期中的那种可以反复播放的“墙纸式”专辑仍然有一点距离。
