禁じ手 椎名林檎

rating: ★★★☆

mark:

至宝 芒に月 W●RK SI・GE・KI 2〇45 松に鶴

An album that exclusively features collaborative tracks with other musicians loses the unified sharpness brought by personal creation, resulting in a diverse yet somewhat scattered overall tone. It is evident that this phase was deliberately defined as an “exploration”: Sheena Ringo actively stepped out of her established creative framework, absorbing new sounds and inspirations through collaborations with different musicians. The first half of the album manages to maintain a relatively stable stylistic focus, while the latter half, with the dense introduction of various elements, begins to feel structurally abrupt. On the textual level, it continues her consistent tension—interweaving metaphorical reflections on power and desire amidst erotic and bodily narratives, ultimately shifting toward an almost maternal, universal stance that attempts to embrace all genders and individuals, ensuring the work retains considerable artistic density in its conceptual dimension.

一张仅收录与其他音乐人合作曲目的专辑，失去个人创作所带来的统一锋芒后，整体气质显得多元却略显散乱。可以看出这一阶段被刻意定义为一次“探索”：椎名林檎主动走出既有的创作框架，通过与不同音乐人合作吸收新的声音与灵感。专辑前半部分尚能维持相对稳定的风格重心，而后半段随着多种元素的密集引入，结构开始显得突兀。文本层面依旧延续她一贯的张力——在情色与肉身叙事之间穿插对权力与欲望的隐喻性思考，最终又转向一种近乎母性化的普世姿态，试图拥抱所有性别与个体，使作品在观念层面仍保持相当的艺术密度。
