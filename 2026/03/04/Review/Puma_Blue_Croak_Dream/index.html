Croak Dream Puma Blue

rating: ★★★★

mark:

Desire Mister Lost Croak Dream (Fool) Jaded

The main body presents an Alt R&B with the dark ambiance of Trip Hop, which can be seen as a microcosm of the genre’s evolutionary path over the past decade: it includes sections that intersect with Indie Rock, building texture with guitar riffs, as well as electronic rhythms infused with techno pulses and glitch sounds with a distorted, slanted quality. At the same time, it blends arrangements rooted in independent folk with bedroom pop expressions that lean toward an atmospheric direction, layered with synthesizers. In comparison, the lo-fi treatment on the album feels somewhat formulaic, lacking sufficient novelty. The overall listening experience is dominated by a soothing rhythm, with restrained and stable vocals, and finely detailed, intricate drum patterns. In the context of a first encounter with the genre, this type of sound design holds strong appeal. Additionally, the subtle arrangements and textural treatments in the titular track evoke certain aesthetic inclinations reminiscent of Radiohead during their Kid A era.

主体呈现出带有 Trip Hop 幽暗气质的 Alt R&B，可以视作该流派近十年演化路径的一个小型汇聚体：既有与 Indie Rock 接轨、以吉他 riff 构建肌理的段落，也有汲取 techno 脉冲的电子律动，以及带有失真倾斜质感的 glitch 声响；同时融合了以独立民谣为底的编排与偏氛围导向、合成器铺陈的卧室流行表达。相较之下，专辑中的 lo-fi 处理反而略显程式化，缺乏足够的新意。整体听感以舒缓节奏为主，人声克制而稳定，鼓点切分细密而精致；若放在初次接触该流派的语境下，这类声音设计具有较强吸引力。此外，同名曲中隐约可辨的编曲与质感处理，也让人联想到 Radiohead 在《Kid A》时期的某些审美取向。
