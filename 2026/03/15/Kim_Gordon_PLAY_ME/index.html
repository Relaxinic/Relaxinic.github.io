PLAY ME Kim Gordon

rating: ★★★☆

mark:

PLAY ME NO HANDS DIRTY TECH NOT TODAY

At 73, Kim Gordon appears to no longer need to care about external evaluations, and her creative work seems exceptionally free and direct. Building on the industrial aesthetic of her early career, her work has shifted toward an experimental hip-hop expression. Compared to the more common rappers in the market, her confidence does not rely on posture or performance but rather resembles an inner strength that has naturally grown from years of creative experience. The album maintains a remarkably flexible layering and texture in its sound design, though when the vocal production takes a backseat, her vocal delivery occasionally feels somewhat lacking in force.

73 岁的 Kim Gordon 似乎已经不再需要在意外界评价，她的创作显得格外自由而直接。作品在延续她早期工业气质的基础上，转向带有实验意味的嘻哈表达，相较于市面上常见的说唱歌手，那种自信并不依赖于姿态或表演，而更像是从长期创作经验中自然生长出来的内在力量。专辑在音效设计上保持了相当灵活的层次与质感，不过当人声制作退到次要位置时，她的人声表达偶尔会显得有些力道不足。
