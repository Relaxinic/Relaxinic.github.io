The Mirror Buck Meek

rating: ★★★★

mark:

Pretty Flowers Can I Mend It? Ring Of Fire Heart In The Mirror Worms Soul Feeling Outta Body

Big Thief guitarist Buck Meek’s new solo album, produced by the band’s drummer and featuring contributions from lead singer Adrianne Lenker. This familiar and close-knit collaboration ensures the entire work retains the warmth and intimacy characteristic of Big Thief. Perhaps because the creator himself is a guitarist, the guitar arrangements on the album are particularly intricate: the transitions between melodic lines and harmonic layers flow naturally, with meticulous attention to detail. At the same time, some unexpected glitch sound effects are subtly embedded in the background, injecting subtle dynamics and interest into otherwise straightforward passages. The overall listening experience is gentle and restrained, yet the lyrics consistently focus on micro-scale pain—those small, specific emotions are repeatedly examined in the vocals, creating an underlying tension beneath the work’s gentle surface.

Big Thief 吉他手 Buck Meek 的全新个人专辑，由乐队鼓手担任制作人，主唱 Adrianne Lenker 亦参与创作。这样熟稔而紧密的合作关系，使整张作品在气质上延续了 Big Thief 一贯的温度与亲密感。或许正因创作者本身出身于吉他手，专辑中的吉他编排显得尤为精细：旋律线与和声层次之间的衔接自然流动，细节处理颇具匠心。与此同时，一些难以预料的 glitch 音效被低调地嵌入背景之中，为原本近乎平铺直叙的段落注入了微妙的动态与趣味。整体听感轻柔克制，但歌词却始终聚焦于微观尺度的苦痛——那些细小而具体的情绪在歌声中被反复凝视，使得作品在温和的表层之下始终潜藏着隐约的张力。
