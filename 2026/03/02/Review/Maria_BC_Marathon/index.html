Marathon Maria BC

rating: ★★★★

mark:

Marathon Peacemaking Rare The sound Sabotage May this rain

Beginning with a rough, distorted opening, Maria constructs a horizontally expansive, hazy, and tranquil folk soundscape. The coarse yet innovative percussion sections and the grainy texture of the guitar tones have clearly undergone meticulous and restrained layering and shaping. The production and mixing are complex, yet beneath the multi-track layering, the overall composition maintains an extremely minimalist and introverted character. This “richness within restraint” also hints at its lasting appeal. Independent folk music centered on uncertain fragility and somber melancholy is not uncommon, but she handles this theme, which could easily become stagnant, with fluidity and luster. However, the conclusion feels somewhat abrupt, slightly undermining the completeness of the overall structure.

由粗粝的失真开场，Maria构建出一个横向延展、迷离而静谧的民谣声场。那些粗粝却新颖的打击声部与富有颗粒感的吉他音色，显然经过了精细而克制的层层塑形；制作与混音的复杂度不低，但在多轨叠加之下，整体仍维持着极简、内敛的气质，这种“克制中的丰富”也预示了其耐听度。围绕不确定的脆弱与低沉的悲情展开的独立民谣并不罕见，但她将这一易于流于滞重的主题处理得流动而有光泽。不过，结尾的收束略显仓促，削弱了整体结构的完成度。
